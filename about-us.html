About Jollibee

Jollibee stands is the Philippines' largest fast-food chain, with a network exceeding 1,500 stores across 17 countries. Keeping strict standards in food quality, service,
and cleanliness, Jollibee offers an array of affordable but high-quality dishes. It is
known for the iconic Chickenjoy, flavorful Yumburger, and hearty Jolly Spaghetti.

Jollibee Foods Corporation (JFC) specializes in developing, operating, and franchising quick-service restaurant brands. Known for its numerous
menu serving customers of all ages and backgrounds.





Jollibee Group Foundation

JGF is the social development arm of the Jollibee Group, one of the fastest growing Asian restaurant companies in the world. JGF promotes improved access to food by implementing programs on agro-entrepreneurship, education and disaster response, together with partner organizations in the Philippines and from other countries.
